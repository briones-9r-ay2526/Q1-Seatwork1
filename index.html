1. Little Women (2019)

Set after the Civil War, the story follows the four March sisters: Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy. Upon watching the film, not only did I find myself reflected in each character, but I also started to love cinema.

Cast

  • Saoirse Ronan (Jo March)
  • Florence Pugh (Amy March)
  • Timothee Chalamet (Theodore Laurence)

2. Black Swan (2010)

Nina is a ballerina whose passion rules every facet of her life. After she gets the main role in their opening production, "Swan Lake," we watch as Nina tests her limits to be perfect.

Cast

  • Natalie Portman (Nina Sayers)
  • Mila Kunis (Lily, the Black Swan)
  • Vincent Cassel (Thomas Leroy)

3. Monsters University (2013)

To achieve his dream of being a Scarer, Mike Wazowski enrolls in Monsters University. Here, he meets peculiar monsters and participates in a school tournament.

Cast

  • Billy Crystal (Mike Wazowski)
  • John Goodman (James P. Sullivan)
  • Steve Buscemi (Randall Boggs)
